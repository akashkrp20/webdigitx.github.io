Some call threaded discussions, discussion boards or discussion groups while a Forum where users share their ideas, thoughts or help by posting text messages on online discussion group. Forums are different from Chat System and it is almost never live and can be read at any time.

Forum is a place where people have the ability to start communication in the form of threads and reply to other people's threads.
